Your Frequently Asked Questions

Do I pay more for my health insurance if I take out insurance through SANTEXPAT rather than directly on the insurer's website?

NO, the rates are the same or cheaper (in some cases SANTEXPAT has negotiated preferential rates for its policyholders thanks to the volume and the trust developed with the insurer) but never more expensive, and SANTEXPAT allows you to check at any time that you benefit from the best health insurance solution according to your profile, your family composition and your country of expatriation;

When does my contract take effect when I take out insurance via SANTEXPAT?

Depending on the insurers and the medical situations, the contract can take effect immediately after validation or within 24 working hours if the medical questionnaire is blank; if the medical questionnaire presents a medical history, the insurer's medical experts may take up to 5 working days to validate your contract and possibly ask you additional questions to better understand your medical history.

What happens if I have a medical history (serious or chronic illnesses)?

The medical experts of the insurers analyze your case and can recommend the acceptance of the contract, the refusal of the contract or a guarantee, or an increase in the premium of the contract.

I have to travel outside my country of expatriation, will I be covered?

Yes for accidents and illnesses requiring emergency care.

Are there waiting periods?

Yes in Maternity (10 months), Dental (3 months for care and 6 months for prostheses), Optics (6 months) and Psychiatry (12 months)
